Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

About This App

The FCTS Booking app is an internal booking management tool operated exclusively by Fleurieu Chauffeur and Tours (ABN 65 468 185 738). This app is used by authorised staff only and is not available to the general public.

Information We Collect

The app collects booking information including client names, contact details, pick-up and drop-off addresses, flight numbers and booking dates. This information is entered manually by FCTS staff for the purpose of managing chauffeur bookings and transfers.

How We Use Information

Information entered into the app is used solely to manage and coordinate chauffeur bookings and transfers for Fleurieu Chauffeur and Tours. Booking records are stored locally on the device and are used exclusively for internal business operations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent or share any personal information with third parties. Booking information is used exclusively for internal business operations of Fleurieu Chauffeur and Tours.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information stored within the app. The app does not transmit personal data over the internet to external servers or third-party services.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by authorised business staff only and is not directed at or intended for use by children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
